# Product Manager @ StartupXYZ

StartupXYZ was a fast-paced FinTech startup where I built a mobile banking app from zero to 100k users.

## Key Achievements

- **100k+ downloads** in the first year
- **4.8 star rating** on app stores
- Reduced customer support tickets by 40% through intuitive UX
- Managed €2M product budget

## My Role

As the first PM, I wore many hats - from writing PRDs to conducting user interviews to coordinating with regulators.

## Challenges & Solutions

The biggest challenge was regulatory compliance. I built strong relationships with compliance teams and created a framework for rapid feature development within banking constraints.

## Technologies Used

- Swift, Kotlin for mobile
- REST APIs, GraphQL
- Mixpanel for analytics
- Jira for sprint management
