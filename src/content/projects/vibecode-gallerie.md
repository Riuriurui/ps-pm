# Vibecode Gallerie

A curated gallery showcasing the intersection of AI assistance and creative coding - projects built with AI tools that maintain human creativity and artistic vision.

## The Concept

"Vibecode" is about using AI as a creative partner while keeping the human soul in the code. This gallery celebrates projects that nail the vibe - where AI accelerates development without sacrificing originality.

## Featured Projects

Each entry in the gallery demonstrates:
- Strong aesthetic direction
- Unique creative voice
- Clever use of AI tools
- Functional, polished end products

## Gallery Categories

- **Generative Art**: AI-assisted visual art and animations
- **Creative Coding**: Interactive web experiences
- **Music Tech**: AI in music production and sound design
- **Experiments**: Wild ideas that push boundaries

## Mission

To inspire and educate developers about the creative possibilities of AI-assisted development.
